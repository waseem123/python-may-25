Operator is any symbol that can perform an operation on data. 
The operation can be anything like 

1. Arithmetic Operators
    (Addition +, Subtraction -, Multiplication *,
    Division /, Modulus %, Floor Division //, Exponential **)

2. Assignment Operators
    (=, +=, -=, *=, /=, %= , //=)

3. Comparison Operators / Relational Operators / Conditional Operators
    (<, >, <=, >=, ==, !=)
    
4. Logical Operators
    (and, or, not)
    
5. Bitwise Operators
    (&, |)
6. Shift Operators
    (<<, >>)
    
7. Identity Operators
    (is, is not)
8. Membership Operators
    (in, not in)
