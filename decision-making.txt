Decision Making - 
The process of deciding the flow of program.

There are two types of Decision Making 
 1. Conditional
    a. if statement

        if <CONDITION>:
            statement 1
            statement 2
            ...
            statment N
    
    b. if-else statement
    c. if - elif - else statement
    d. Nested Conditions

 2. Choice based
    a. match statement
